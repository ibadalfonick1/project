Culinary Art Catering is an award-winning, full-service Dallas catering company known for extraordinary taste, design, and five-star service. For over twenty years, we've brought unforgettable experiences to life for clients across Dallas, Plano, Frisco, Addison, and surrounding North Texas communities.

Whether you're planning a wedding, bar or bat mitzvah, corporate meeting, open house, or private celebration, our team of seasoned professionals will collaborate with you to design a completely personalized event—tailored to your vision, your style, and your budget.

Chef-Owned Hospitality

Rooted In Culinary Excellence, Driven By Hospitality

As a chef-owned catering company, our foundation is culinary, but our purpose is deeply personal. We're not here for chefs to dazzle with ego—we're here to make you shine. Our goal is for your guests to walk away amazed—not just by the food, but by your incredible taste in choosing the right caterer.

At Culinary Art, we are rooted in the old-fashioned values of true hospitality. Every gesture is thoughtful. Every detail is intentional. Every guest experience is crafted with care. We believe hospitality is an art, and we treat it as such—honoring tradition while embracing innovation.
