Our People

Meet The Team

Liuda

Liuda has over 20 years experience working at high end salons. As a colorist she is a popular choice for blondes but also loves working with the full spectrum of tones and colors.

Christina

As a licensed hairstylist for over a decade, Christina brings a bubbly personality to The Salon California. Growing up by the ocean influenced her passion for beach blondes.

Kelly

Kelly's passion for color first pulled her into art school, and her passion for helping people feel beautiful led her to the beauty industry. After 25 years, she still loves learning and evolving.

Danielle

Danielle is a local, energetic senior stylist with years of experience in cuts and color. She specializes in making people beautiful and leaving with a smile.

Aries

Aries is a passionate esthetician focused on helping clients look and feel their best, with experience in lash lifts, brow laminations, Hydrafacials, microdermabrasion, and body treatments.
